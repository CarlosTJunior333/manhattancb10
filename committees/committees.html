The Committee works to increase the public’s level of access to information about the arts in the community; reinforces pride in Harlem’s creativity and cultural expression; encourages the creation of public/private partnerships in order to make public spaces available for local artists to exhibit their work; forms collaborations with art and cultural institutions to enhance the community’s access to the arts; and encourages the use of art in public spaces (e.g. parks, gardens, lobbies, libraries, etc.).

Meets the 2nd Monday of every month at 6:30 pm The meeting agendas are posted online in advance of each meeting (see calendar)
